The app (macOS)

Just Before The Meeting is a menu bar application that can read your Google Calendar when you choose to sign in. Sign-in uses Google OAuth. Access is limited to read-only calendar scope (calendar.readonly). OAuth tokens are stored in the macOS Keychain on your computer. Calendar data is used on your device to schedule countdowns and is not transmitted to servers operated by the app developer.

When you use Google services, Google’s privacy policy applies to data processed by Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
