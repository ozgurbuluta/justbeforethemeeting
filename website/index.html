Just Before The Meeting

A tiny Mac app that lives in your menu bar, watches Google Calendar, and plays your news-style theme with a pulsing countdown right before the events you care about.

Download for Mac (DMG)

macOS 13+. Prefer a signed, notarized build from GitHub Releases.

What it does

  • Background menu bar app — no Dock icon; always within reach.
  • Google Calendar — OAuth sign-in, polls your calendars, respects filters.
  • Custom sound — bring your own news sting (mp3/m4a) or bundle default_theme.
  • Pulsing countdown — red/orange timer in the menu bar; click to dismiss early.
  • Per-event control — rules (all / video / keyword) plus toggles for upcoming items.

How it works

  1. Download the DMG, open it, and drag Just Before The Meeting into Applications.
  2. Launch the app from your menu bar (JBTM icon), then choose Connect Google Calendar and complete sign-in in your browser.
  3. Open Settings to choose your theme sound, countdown timing, and which events count.
  4. Keep the app running so it can refresh your calendar and show the countdown before meetings.
